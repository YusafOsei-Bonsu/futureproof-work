Places to Eat

Pieminister

56 Upper Ground
South Bank
0207 928 5755

pieminister.co.uk

ATTENTION ALL PIE LOVERS... Pie Minister is now serving SWEET pies! Today I sampled a pear and chocolate pie. It was filled with poached pear rum and chocolate in a similar toasty pastry as their savory pies. It was served pipping hot (so don't burn yourself)! So satisfying and a nice sweet treat when you're in the neighborhood. They currently only have three flavors: Adam's Apple Pie (apples & raspberry), Lovely Pear Pie (pear & chocolate), and Toffee Apple pie. I can't wait to try them all.


Battersea Park

28 The Market
Covent Garden
0207 240 9566

batterseapiestation.co.uk

This place is my go to pie place every time I'm in London. I always try to stay in Covent Garden just so I can come here. This is my basis of the perfect meat pie with the pleasingly firm crusts and sides of mash and roasted potatoes are so delicious.


Camden Bakery

94 Camden High Street
Euston
0207 387 4947

camdenbakery.co.uk

Great bakery. Just had probably the best donut I've ever eaten. There not the American type (i.e Krispy Cream) but traditional English donut.
It's not very Artisan/trendy type of place but the food is excellent. Looking at everything else on offer it all looked good from the soup to sweets to sandwiches. Great place.


Rinkoff Bakeries

224 Jubilee Street
Whitechapel
0207 791 4909

rinkoffbakeries.co.uk

Good bakery selling both bread, pastries and cronuts. Their croissants didn't look the best I had seen but the cronuts looked really good and the one that I had was great.
