1. Check you're alive.
2. Get out of bed.
3. Go to the bathroom.
4. Pickup toothbrush.
5. Water on the toothbrush by turning on tap (for 3secs).
6. Pickup toothpaste and place a little bit of paste on toothbrush.
7. Water the paste-topped toothbrush again for 3secs.
8. Turn the water off.
9. Close the toothpaste and place it by the sink.
10. Put the toothbrush in your mouth.
11. Brush all teeth in up-down motion for 2mins.
12. PLace brush by sink.
13. Turn the cold-water tap on.
14. Take water with both hands together.
15. Open mouth and place the water into mouth using hands.
